Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Roulette Helper - Predictor ("the App") handles your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Data Storage

All data you enter in the App — including roulette numbers, machine names, and finance tracking entries — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services such as:

  • Analytics or tracking tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud databases or servers
  • Social media integrations

Permissions

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all App data at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since the App does not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers, most GDPR provisions do not apply. However, we want to confirm the following:

  • No personal data is collected or processed by us.
  • No data is transferred outside your device or to any third country.
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used.
  • You have full control over your data — you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you specific rights regarding your personal information. Since the App does not collect, sell, or share any personal information, the following applies:

  • We do not sell your personal information to any third party.
  • We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • We do not collect categories of personal information as defined by the CCPA.
  • You may exercise your right to deletion at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
